The Oregon Trail
This program simulates a trip over the Oregon Trail from Independence, Missouri to Oregon in 1847.

Your family will cover the 2040 mile Oregon Trail in 5-6 months---if you make it alive.

You have saved $900 to spend for the trip, and you've just paid $200 for a wagon.

You will need to spend the rest of your money on the following items:
  • OXEN - You can spend $200-$300 on your team. The more you spend, the faster you will go because you will have better animals.
  • FOOD - The more you have the less chance there is of getting ill.
  • AMMUNITION - $1 buys 50 bullets for attacks by animals and bandits, and for hunting food.
  • CLOTHING - This is especially needed because of the cold weather you will encounter while you are crossing the mountains.
  • MISCELLANEOUS SUPPLIES - This includes medicine and other things you will need for sickness and for emergency repairs.
